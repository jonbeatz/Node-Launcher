# 🔋 PHASE 2: CORE ENGINE ACTIVATION (PM2 & IPC)

**Objective:** Bridge the "Studio Dark" UI with the underlying Windows 11 hardware layer via Electron IPC and PM2.

---

## 🛰️ 1. IPC HANDSHAKE (Vader Shield)
- **File**: `src/preload/preload.js`
- **Task**: Define the `window.vpeAPI` methods. Ensure a strictly typed bridge for:
  - `getProjects()`: Returns the list from your `projects.json`.
  - `startProject(id)` / `stopProject(id)`: Links to PM2 programmatic API.
  - `nukeProject(id)`: Triggers the deletion of `node_modules` and re-install.

---

## ⚙️ 2. PM2 PROGRAMMATIC CONTROLLER
- **File**: `src/main/pm2-manager.js`
- **Logic**: 
  - Initialize `pm2.connect()` on app startup.
  - Implement a watcher that streams `stdout` and `stderr` to the **Log Drawer** via the `pty` bridge.
  - **Vader Optimization**: Ensure CPU affinity for PM2 processes is aware of the **9700x** high-performance cores.

---
